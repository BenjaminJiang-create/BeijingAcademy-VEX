About Us

A student-led VEX Robotics program at Beijing Academy, focused on engineering excellence, collaboration, and real-world problem solving through competitive robotics.

Team 30721 / 3696 / 6353 / 20139

The Beijing Academy VEX Robotics Team is a student-led engineering program consisting of multiple competitive teams, including 30721, 3696, 6353, and 20139. With strong support from Beijing Academy, our team integrates structured training, independent design, and competitive experience.

Students engage in the complete engineering process—from design and programming to testing and iteration—while developing collaboration skills and a deep understanding of real-world problem solving through robotics competitions.
